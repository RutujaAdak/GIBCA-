Built

Our products are HITACHI and COOLWAVE, which are manufactured in
a very high tech environment, developed considering the extreme
temperatures of the middle east.

We provide our customers with best-in-class products that are
particularly suited for the region, and are built to last.

Design

At GIBCA we create product designs, using methodical and efficient
design processes keeping the current needs of our customer base in
mind. Moreover, our experts design cost-effective and energy-efficient
products based on factors such as climate, building configuration,
orientation, space usage and zoning.

Our overall goal is to support our customers not only in the delivery of
the product but to give them a solution that saves their time, money
and energy year on year. When a customer interacts with us, they can
be ensured that they will be provided real-time efficient solutions and
a fruitful service experience that goes beyond just installation.
