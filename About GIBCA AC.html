About GIBCA

Integrity, Teamwork and
Innovation.

The GIBCA Air Conditioning Division was established in 1976. Recognizing the demand for quality air conditioners for the harsh local climate, the company secured an exclusive distribution agreement with Hitachi Ltd, Japan in 1979 for their Central Air Conditioning Equipment. With that GIBCA also launched another air conditioning brand called Cool air, exclusively to cater to the needs of the locals in Dubai.

Moreover, our professional services, product availability and extensive warranty coverage provided at competitive market prices have led to GIBCA’s Air Conditioning Division being seen as a leading force in the Air Conditioning Industry over the world.

Our long-term goal is to become a one-stop location for a full range of quality air conditioners and air conditioning equipment, having the most efficient engineering support and services.

Our Heritage

The GIBCA Group

The GIBCA Group of Companies was founded in 1975, immediately after the
formation of the United Arab Emirates. Where the management was quick to
see the vast potential in participating in the development of a young nation.

Rest is history, as we set out to become a major entrepreneurial and business
force in the Gulf; creating major opportunities and extensive development in
the areas of manufacturing, contracting, project development, overseas
investment and distribution, using our expertise and long-standing experience
of conducting business.

Further, as the company grew, we were approached by many international
corporations to create exciting business collaborations, helping us expand our
footing in the world.

Currently, as a group, GIBCA is continuously expanding and diversifying its
activities in UAE’s ever-growing manufacturing and trading sectors, through
wholly-owned companies, subsidiaries and joint partnerships, covering a wide
spectrum of products,
